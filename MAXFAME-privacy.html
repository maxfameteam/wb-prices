MAXFAME Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy applies to the MAXFAME Pinterest automation app, used solely for publishing product pins to the official MAXFAME Pinterest account (@maxfame_ru).

What data we collect

This application does not collect any personal data from third-party users. The app is used exclusively for internal automation purposes by MAXFAME team members. The only data processed is product information (article numbers, titles, descriptions, images) owned by MAXFAME.

Why we use it

The app accesses the Pinterest API solely to automate the publishing of product pins to the MAXFAME business account. No data is shared with third parties, sold, or used for advertising purposes.

Cookies and analytics

This application does not use cookies or any analytics tools. No tracking of any kind is performed.

Data deletion

Since we do not collect personal data from users, there is nothing to delete. If you have any concerns, please contact us and we will respond within 30 days.
